# Style Guide: "Amigos da Fauna" Awareness Project

This guide establishes the visual identity standards based on the project's communication materials to ensure consistency across new assets.

---

## 1. Color Palette
The palette is composed of earthy tones, natural greens, and institutional colors that reference Brazilian fauna and flora.

### Primary Colors (Typography & Branding)
* **Fauna Brown:** `#5D4037` (Main titles and logos).
* **Forest Green:** `#4A5D23` (Guest names and environmental education details).
* **Terracotta Orange:** `#D38345` (Subtitles and emphasis).

### Background & Support Colors
* **Recycled Paper Beige:** `#F5F1E3` (Predominant background color with light texture).
* **Nature Yellow:** `#FBC02D` (Used in details, such as the jaguar texture in the logo).
* **Institutional Blue:** `#005DAA` (Specifically for the Unileste logo application).

---

## 2. Typography
The project utilizes a mix of modern sans-serif fonts with organic/handwritten styles.

### Impact Titles
* **Style:** Sans-serif, Extra-Bold, All-Caps.
* **Font Suggestions:** *Montserrat Black*, *Arial Black*, or *Futura Extra Bold*.
* **Application:** Main titles like "AMIGOS DA FAUNA".

### Organic / Thematic Titles
* **Style:** Display, slightly irregular, with a "stamp" or stylized manual writing look.
* **Font Suggestions:** *Keep on Truckin'*, *Caveat* (for a thinner touch), or *Chewy*.
* **Application:** Specific titles like "ARARA-CANINDÉ" or "ANiMAiS".

### Body Text & Subtitles
* **Style:** Sans-serif, Rounded or simple Geometric.
* **Font Suggestions:** *Quicksand (Bold)*, *Varela Round*, or *Nunito*.
* **Application:** Supporting phrases like "como começou" or "Você conhece os 3R's?".

---

## 3. Visual Support Elements
To maintain the visual language, new materials should include:
1.  **Background Texture:** Use of a lightly grainy background resembling recycled paper or natural fiber.
2.  **Animal Illustrations:** Use of realistic or high-quality vector illustrations (e.g., Capybara, Macaw, Snake).
3.  **Eco Icons:** 3D recycling symbols and sustainability seals in lime green and brown tones.
